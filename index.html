    How to reach?

  • IIITDM Kancheepuram is located in Kandigai, in Vandalur-Kelambakkam road, 18 kms from Tambaram.
  • The best way to reach is by bus.
  • 55D takes you directly to IIITDM’s doorsteps.
  • For other buses, Tambaram → Kandigai (bus) → IIITDMK (Auto at Kandigai bus stop).
  • Buses are also available from Vandalur gate/zoo, which brings you straight to Kandigai.

    Rules of stay

    Fare : Rs.150 per day(Non AC Accomodation)

  • Smoking, drinking and other drug consumptions are strictly prohibited and necessary action will be taken by the institute if a participant is found to be in possession of these items.
  • Any damage to institution facilities and property provided to the participants would result in serious action and necessary reimbursement charges would have to be dealt by the participants who are found guilty.
  • Participants are expected to not create any kind of nuisance which might trouble other participants in the room.
  • The college will not be responsible for any damage or loss of property or valuables stored in places of accommodation.

  • The decision of organisers is final and binding in case of any dispute.
